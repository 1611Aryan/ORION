About

Tech Fest of TIET, Patiala

Orion is an Annual Intra-College Technical Summit of TIET, a festival which was started years ago and is now a working society at Thapar. We aim to promote the technical developments taking place throughout the world, making efforts to implement some of them too. We have a vision to tell every student about modern advancements, help students realise their potential to work. It is a Tech Fest, aiming to bring out the best of people, with only motive “learning”.
